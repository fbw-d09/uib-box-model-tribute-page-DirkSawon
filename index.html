Frank Herbert

"[...] Well, now it's writing time and now I'll write."
  • 1920 - Born in Tacoma, Washington
  • 1938 - Moved to Salem, Oregon
  • 1942 - Military Service WWII
  • 1949 - Moved to California
  • 1952 - His science fiction stories began appearing in magazines
  • 1955 - Herbert's first novel The Dragon in the Sea was released
  • 1965 - The novel Dune was published in 1965, which spearheaded the Dune franchise
  • 1965 - 1985 - Five additional Dune novels were published
